ABOUT AL NAJMA AL FAREEDA GROUP

Welcome to the world of inspiration. There’s a lot to know about Al Fareeda Group, so let's start with the facts. We provide facilities, management solutions delivering the broadest range of services that can be found in one place: from integrated facilities management, to sustainability solutions and much more. At Alfareeda Group, we not only build and maintain facilities but we deliver our promise through.

We are specialist in offering customized solutions to commercial, residential, industrial, hospital and educational facilities through our unique solutions. We provide world class FM and recruitment services that add value to our customers. We invest in our people, in facilities, in technologies and in market awareness.

We believe in making a difference, being open to change, meeting challenges and learning from our experiences. Our objective is to know our global consumers and develop Services that fit their needs. Our inspiration comes from listening to our customers and providing solutions for any FM and Recruitment issues they require, through the following companies.

Our in-house expertise means that wherever you are and whatever your FM and recruitment requirements are, we can provide the right solution cost-effectively. Dubai has always been leading the UAE and the GCC when it comes to building flagship projects, buildings and facilities. We, at Alfareeda Group, are aware that the facilities management industry has to evolve and go to the next level to cater for the boom as a result of EXPO 2020.

With a humble beginning in 2006, Al Fareeda Group started as a cleaning company and has expanded rapidly to offer complete facility management solutions. In a short span of time, Al Fareeda Group is recognized globally for its excellence as a result of the enormous efforts of staff, workers, and the support of suppliers and customers.

As Facilities requires the highest levels of resource optimization, cost-effectiveness and occupant well-being, our facility management team has been developing customized solutions for operating the buildings with a holistic view of the asset life-cycle in a sustainable way. Facility Management Solutions are available for commercial, residential, industrial, hospital and educational facilities. With the increasing trend toward the development of higher density buildings, these customized solutions ensures that the assets are well managed and the property's value is maintained.
